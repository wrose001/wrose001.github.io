About Me

Hello! My name is Weston Rose, and I have been working in the field of higher education administration for almost a decade after earning my master's degree in English in 2010. After a few years of teaching Composition & Rhetoric to college freshmen and working part-time as an acadmic advisor, I began working as a Media & Communications Specialist in the Office of Graduate Studies at Sam Houston State University. In addition to editing a magazine about graduate student research, one of my responsibilities was to update my office's web section as a content publisher, using a content management system called WebCMS. I became very interested in learning to code at this point because while I could create, edit, and delete webpages, I found that using the WYSIWYG mode limited my abilities; I started researching HTML and making small changes to the source code.
